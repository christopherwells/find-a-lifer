### Session 2 - 2026-02-07

**Feature Completed:** #2 - Species metadata API loads correctly

**What was accomplished:**
- Created backend/build_species_metadata.py:
  - Downloads and parses eBird taxonomy CSV (10,994 world species)
  - Merges with avibase.csv (2,511 regional species with invasion/conservation data)
  - Filters to 2,490 species present in North America/Central America/Caribbean
  - Computes difficulty scores based on geographic spread, invasion status, conservation status
  - Detects restricted-range species (present in <=2 regions as Native/Present)
  - Generates eBird URLs for all species
- Species metadata includes ALL required fields:
  - Core: species_id, speciesCode, comName, sciName, familyComName, taxonOrder
  - Enrichment: invasionStatus (3 values), conservStatus (8 values), difficultyScore, difficultyLabel (4 levels), isRestrictedRange
  - URLs: ebirdUrl, photoUrl
- 143 unique taxonomic families represented
- Data enrichment stats:
  - Invasion: 1851 Native, 546 Rare/Accidental, 93 Introduced
  - Conservation: 1526 Least Concern, 125 Near Threatened, 89 Vulnerable, 56 Endangered, 30 Critically Endangered, 650 Unknown
  - Difficulty: 1633 Very Hard, 486 Easy, 207 Hard, 164 Moderate
  - 1107 restricted range species
